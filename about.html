Ali Raza

I develop applications on iOS, Rails and .NET framework. Like every other Geek I am a huge fan of Sci-Fi and Video Games. I love Rock music, from classics like Guns n Roses and Nirvana to MCR and Fountains of Wayne. Lets see what else, Oh I am a caffeine lover and enjoy staring for long periods of time into the abyss.
